What is CEED?

CEED is a national-level postgraduate design entrance exam conducted by IIT Bombay — the gateway to M.Des programs at IITs, IISc Bangalore, and IIITDM Jabalpur.

It assesses design creativity, reasoning, and problem-solving. CEED opens careers in industrial design, UX, animation, visual communication, and more.

No age limit and unlimited attempts make CEED accessible to working professionals and fresh graduates alike. Any stream graduate with a 3-year degree or diploma is eligible.
