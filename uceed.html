WHAT IS UCEED?

UCEED — Undergraduate Common Entrance Examination for Design — is a national-level entrance exam conducted by IIT Bombay. It is the gateway to top design programs across India's most prestigious institutions.

A B.Des from an IIT opens doors to careers in product design, UX/UI, graphic design, animation, and industrial design — with top companies and global opportunities waiting.

IIT Bombay IIT Delhi IIT Guwahati IIT Hyderabad IIT Roorkee IIITDM Jabalpur

UCEED evaluates a candidate's aptitude, visualization, creativity, and design skills. It is not just another entrance exam — it tests how creatively and analytically a student thinks, and how observant they are of the world around them.

Who Can Apply

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

A candidate must simultaneously fulfil all three criteria to appear for UCEED. Students from all streams — Science, Commerce, Arts & Humanities — are eligible.

Criterion Description
Age Limit Criteria to be checked in the appearing year's official brochure.
Number of Attempts Maximum of two attempts, and that too in consecutive years. UCEED score is valid for one year and only for admissions in the same academic year.
Qualifying Exam Must have appeared for Class XII (or equivalent) for the first time in 2024 or 2025. Students from all streams (Science, Commerce, Arts & Humanities) are eligible.

SYLLABUS

Understanding the syllabus is the first step to cracking UCEED. Here's what each part covers.

Part A Syllabus
CBT
Visualization & Spatial
Ability to visualize and transform 2D and 3D shapes and their relationships
Scientific & Practical Knowledge
Understanding of basic science and everyday objects
Observation & Design Sensitivity
Attention to detail, hidden properties, critical thinking, analysis, inference
Environment & Society
Awareness of environmental, social, and cultural contexts in design
Analytical & Logical Reasoning
Ability to interpret qualitative and quantitative information
Language
Proficiency in reading and understanding Standard English
Creativity
Use of analogies, metaphors, signs, and symbols
Part B Syllabus
Pen & Paper
Drawing
Ability to sketch products, people, or scenes with proportion, line quality, composition, and shading
Design Aptitude
Ability to practically respond to problems with creativity, empathy, and contextual awareness
