Our Vision

WHY WE EXIST

At My Design Academia, our vision is to serve as a constant support system for aspiring designers, providing the training, resources, and mentorship they need to thrive in this competitive field.

We take pride in our team of passionate and dedicated educators — alumni from NID, IIT, NIFT and top Architecture institutes — brought together by a shared mission to guide and empower the next generation of designers.

Visiting Faculty

DECADES OF EXPERIENCE

We also invite visiting faculty with decades of experience, many of whom have served as professors and jurors at leading design institutions.

Their combined academic and industry insights offer students a well-rounded, real-world perspective on design — the kind that no textbook alone can provide.
