What is UCEED?

UCEED (Undergraduate Common Entrance Examination for Design) is a national-level exam conducted by IIT Bombay — the gateway to B.Des programs at IITs and IIITDM Jabalpur.

It evaluates aptitude, visualization, creativity, and design thinking. A B.Des from an IIT opens careers in product design, UX/UI, graphic design, animation, and industrial design.

The Exam

What is CEED?

CEED (Common Entrance Exam for Design) is a national-level postgraduate design entrance exam conducted by IIT Bombay. It is the gateway to M.Des programs at IITs, IISc Bangalore, and IIITDM Jabalpur.

It assesses design creativity, reasoning, and problem-solving for postgraduate design. CEED opens careers in industrial design, UX, animation, visual communication, and more at India's premier institutions.

No age limit and unlimited attempts make CEED accessible to working professionals and fresh graduates alike. Any stream graduate with a 3-year degree or diploma is eligible to apply.
